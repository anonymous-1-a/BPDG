This is the interview process for Capgemini Technology Services India Limited. The first step is the technical team interview, where they conduct a technical round. If the candidate successfully passes the technical round, the HR team proceeds to conduct an HR round. If the candidate also passes the HR Round, the HR Team creates the Final Selection List and adds the candidate's name to it. According to this list, an offer letter is sent to the candidate. If the candidate does not qualify and is rejected, they should be informed about the outcome.
