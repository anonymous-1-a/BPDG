Upon receiving a purchase order in the ERP System of the Seller, the system will proceed to check the stock availability. If the product is not in stock, the warehouse and distribution worker of the seller will retrieve it from the warehouse. If the product is available, the ERP system will then check the availability of raw materials. In case Supplier 2 provides the raw materials, the worker will request and obtain them from Supplier 2. If Supplier 1 provides the raw materials, the worker will request and obtain them from Supplier 1. Subsequently, the worker will manufacture the product. Once these steps are completed, the Sales team of the seller will confirm the order and will issue an invoice, receive the payment. Meanwhile, warehouse distribution worker will obtain the shipping address for product shipment. After the above process is completed, the Sales team of the seller will archive order.
