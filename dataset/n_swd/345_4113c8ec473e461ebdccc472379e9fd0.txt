The customer service assistant at the Photo Studio prepares the invoice. Then, they select the payment type. If the payment is made by card, the customer service assistant requests payment from the bank. If the payment is not authorized, they continue to request payment in a loop. Once the payment is authorized, they confirm the payment and exit the process. If the payment is made by cash, the customer service assistant pays in cash. If the payment is made by bank transfer, they continue to send studio account information and confirm payment . Finally, the customer service assistant updates the payment confirmation.
