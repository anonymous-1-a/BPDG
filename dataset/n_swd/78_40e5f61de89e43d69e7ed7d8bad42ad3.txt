The custodian of Försäkringskassan selects the child's claim file. Following this, the custodian fills in the claim period dates. The Parental Leave System of Försäkringskassan then verifies the child's relation to the custodian and assesses the claim's validity. If the claim is invalid, the process loops; if valid, the process concludes. Next, the custodian reviews and signs the claim. Finally, the Parental Leave System of Försäkringskassan registers the claim.
