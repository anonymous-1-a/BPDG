Upon the warehouse & distribution department of Nicolaus Copernicus University in Torun receiving a purchase order, the warehouse & distribution department  will check the order against the stock using the ERP system. If the product is available, it will be retrieved from the warehouse. If not, the ERP system will verify raw material availability, and then the warehouse & distribution department will proceed to manufacture the product. Once this process is completed, the sales department will confirm the order. Subsequently, the sales department will issue an invoice and await payment, while the warehouse & distribution department will ship the product. Concurrently, the sales department will confirm the purchase and archive the order.
