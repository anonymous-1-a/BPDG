The photographer from Photosynthesis Studio is assigned for booking. They will then check the equipment requirements. If special equipment is needed, the Photosynthesis Studio's equipment team will provide it; otherwise, they will move on. Next, the photographer will check the shooting location. If it's an on-location session, they will drive there; if it's an in-studio session, they will proceed. Following this, the photographer will prepare for the shoot and then conduct the video/photo session. Then, any special requirements will be handled by the Photosynthesis Studio team, if special equipment is needed, the Photosynthesis Studio's equipment team will move onbringing back special equipment; otherwise,  they will move on. Finally, the photographer will upload the photos and videos before cleaning up the photos.
