The information system of Mighty Jane Doe receives an order. Following that, the system sends a confirmation email to the customer. The order fulfillment department of Mighty Jane Doe then packs the order. Subsequently, the order fulfillment department of Mighty Jane Doe check whether the shipment requires bubble protection. If bubble protection is necessary, the Order Fulfillment Department of Mighty Jane Doe will add it; otherwise, they will skip this step. The order is then passed to the warehouse. The warehouse of Mighty Jane Doe checks whether the post should be normal or fast courier. If normal, they fill in the post label. If fast courier service is chosen, they request a quote from the contracted courier and prepare the courier paperwork. Finally, the warehouse combines the paperwork with the package for delivery pickup.
