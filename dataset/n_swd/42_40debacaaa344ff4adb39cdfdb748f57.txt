The ACME AG Human Resources Department receives the application. They then send an acknowledgement of receipt. the head of ACME AG Human Resources verifies the applicant. If the application status is accepted, the applicant is invited to a job interview. If rejected, a rejection notice is sent. The applicant is then invited to an interview. After the interview, an employment agreement is drawn up. If the applicant meets the requirements, an employment agreement is sent. If rejected, a rejection notice is sent. Upon receipt of a signed employment contract, it will be acknowledged.
