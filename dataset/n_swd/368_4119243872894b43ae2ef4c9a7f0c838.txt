Sales of Oil Bit Inc. receives a sales inquiry, gathers requirements, searches the database, creates a sales order, and forwards the sales order. Once the project engineering team at Oil Bit Inc. receives the sales order, they create manufacturing instructions. In parallel, the team prepares iron ore and casts rotor teeth. Afterward, the project engineering team pours the drill bit base and forwards the materials. Technical Services of Oil Bit Inc. then receives the materials, assembles the drill bit, tests it, and finally ships the drill bit.
