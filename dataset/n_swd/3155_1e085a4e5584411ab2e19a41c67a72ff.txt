The IT system at Hestháls records activities every working day. Then, it receives inspection documentation. At 10 pm daily, the system logs the activities. The vehicle department manager at Hestháls checks the recordings every morning. The IT system transmits the records. The reception area supervisor at Hestháls checks the inspection document. If errors are found, the supervisor oversees corrections. Finally, the IT system records the transmitted recordings unless no errors are identified.
