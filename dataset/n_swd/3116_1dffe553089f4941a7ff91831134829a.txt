After receiveed the order from customer,  if the customer is dissatisfied and requests a return, the ERP system for order returns will validate the request. If the request is declined, the ERP system will send a denial confirmation and complete the process. If approved, the ERP system will confirm the approval, provide return information to the customer, arrange for pick-up, and assign a delivery partner. The delivery partner will be notified of the pick-up details. The customer will repack the item, and the delivery partner will pick it up. The item will be delivered to the warehouse for confirmation. The ERP system will issue a refund to the customer, who will receive it to conclude the process.
