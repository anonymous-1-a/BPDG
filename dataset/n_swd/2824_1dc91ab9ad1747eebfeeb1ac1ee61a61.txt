The worker at Viata Logistics receives the order. They process, prepare, sort, and label the products. Then, they greet first and second-time customers with a card and scan the products. After checking the products, if any issues are found, the warehouse manager at Viata Logistics handles the faulty order. The warehouse manager inspects the products and decides whether to send them or order missing items from the supplier. Upon receiving the order from the suppliers, the warehouse manager sends it back for processing. If the products are in good condition, the process proceeds to Step 3 and concludes.

Next, the worker at Viata Logistics processes the order by selecting the appropriate box, packing delicate contents, and conducting a final inspection of the order tags and the bill. Finally, they pack the order and place it in the respective delivery carts.
